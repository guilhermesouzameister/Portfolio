I'm Guilherme Rafael — a 3D artist and visual designer who blends organic forms with futuristic aesthetics. My work lives at the intersection of nature and technology, inspired by Solar Punk visions and Y2K futurism.

From ad productions to product visualizations, I bring ideas to life through motion, form, and light — always with a deep respect for the natural world and a passion for pushing creative boundaries.
